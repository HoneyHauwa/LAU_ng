ABOUT US

As LAU representatives in Nigeria, we support Nigerian students aspiring to study at Lincoln American University (LAU) . Our roles include promoting the university, providing detailed information about programs, and guiding students through the application and visa processes. We assist with filling out forms, gathering necessary documents, and navigating the requirements to make the process smooth. We also help students prepare for their journey through pre-departure orientations covering cultural differences and accommodation options. Additionally, we provide financial planning support, including information on fees and scholarships. Our ongoing assistance ensures that Nigerian students feel well-prepared and supported throughout their study abroad experience.

About LAU

Lincoln American University (LAU) is a private medical school located in Guyana, South America. It's recognized as one of the top medical universities in the Caribbean, providing a comprehensive medical education program. The medium of instruction is English, and the university follows a curriculum based on US standards. This makes it an attractive option for students around the world.

WHY STUDY AT LAU

Program Structure: LAU offers a five-year MD program divided into three phases: Pre-medical (1 year), Preclinical (2 years), and Clinical Rotations (2 years). Students have the option to complete clinical rotations in the USA, which enhances their global exposure and clinical skills.

Accreditations: The university is listed in the World Directory of Medical Schools (WDOMS), recognized by the National Accreditation Council of Guyana (NAC), and has certifications from the Foundation for Advancement of International Medical Education and Research (FAIMER) and the Education Commission for Foreign Medical Graduates (ECFMG). It is also recognized by the Medical Council of India (MCI).

Facilities and Support: LAU provides well-equipped classrooms, laboratory-based education, and hostel facilities with amenities. The cost of living in Guyana is relatively low, making it affordable for students. The university also offers a multicultural environment with a community that includes many individuals of Indian origin.

LAU is a good option for those seeking affordable medical education with opportunities for international clinical rotations and a curriculum aligned with US medical standards.
